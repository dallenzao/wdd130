Content

Home page

The home page will give users an idea about what to expect on the site, a quick overview about what the company offers and links to the different areas of the website. The home page will also have a catchy headline, a logo and some images that showcase the quality and variety of products and services available. The home page will aim to attract and retain the attention of potential customers and encourage them to explore the site further.

Images for the Home page

Custom PCs / Repairs

There will be 2 pages for 2 separate services. One service will be to configure a custom PC, while another will be to request a repair on a device. The Custom PC page will give possible configurations based on different categories such as gaming, work or entertainment. It will also have a form with some questions that ask for the users contact info, budget, size, color, specific preferences on parts and brands etc. The form will allow users to customize their PC according to their needs and preferences. The Repairs page will give a list of possible devices that can be repaired such as laptops, tablets or smartphones. It will also have another subpage within it that will give some pricing on common repairs such as phone screens and batteries. The pricing will be transparent and competitive with other repair shops in the area. There will also be a contact form requesting information on the device model number, what happened and what repair is wanting to be completed as well as their contact information. The form will help users get an estimate of the cost and time required for their repair.

Images for the Page 2

Contact Us / About Us

The Contact Us page and About Us page are two different pages with similar layouts. The Contact Us page will include a generic form requesting information about what they need such as feedbacks or inquiries as well as their info so we can get back to them promptly. This page will also include a direct email address and phone number for more urgent matters as well as an address for the physical location of the business with a map showing directions. The About Us page will include a little blurb about the companys history, mission, vision and values with some pictures that illustrate its culture, team work environment etc.
